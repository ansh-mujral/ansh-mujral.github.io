Hi, I'm Ansh Mujral

I'm a Data Science student at UC San Diego with a passion for uncovering the stories hidden in data. Whether it's developing machine learning models, analyzing trends, or exploring innovative research, I thrive on tackling challenges that make a real-world impact. Throughout my journey, I've worked on diverse projects, from predicting recipe ratings and analyzing gaming skill gaps in Valorant tournaments to researching adaptive machine learning methods like Test-Time Adaptation to improve model performance under distribution shifts. These experiences have allowed me to combine technical skills with creative problem-solving to drive meaningful results. When I'm not deep in code, you'll find me on the basketball court, playing piano, or in the gym. I'm also a huge fan music, which remind me of the power of creativity and self-expression.
